Perfect Scrollbar is a nice library showing a customizable scrollbar on every browser, on every operating system. You can add it to ngx-extended-pdf-viewer with a few lines of code.

Caveat: I suspect the library has been abandoned. I'm going to remove both the demo and the support soon.

In this demo, the distinguishing feature of the perfect scrollbar is that it gets a bit wider when the mouse hovers over it. If the mouse is outside the PDF file, the scrollbar vanishes completely.
