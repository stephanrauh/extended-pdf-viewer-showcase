Since version 24.2.0, users can modify the page order of the PDF file:

  • You can swap two pages in the thumbnail view. Just drag and drop a thumbnail up or down.
  • The toolbar offers buttons to move the current page up or down.

By default, drag-and-drop reordering is deactivated. You must enable it with pdfDefaultOptions.enablePageReordering = true.

The toolbar buttons are also invisible by default. You must enable them with [movePageButton]="true" (or any other of the ResponsiveVisibility attributes).
