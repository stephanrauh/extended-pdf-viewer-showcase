The PDF viewer accepts different types of input: URLs, BLOBS, UInt8Array, and Base64 encoded strings. The first three can simply be passed to the attribute [src].

Base64 encoded files, in contrast, have to be passed to the attribute [base64src].

Caveat: I've seen developers populating both [src] and [base64src]. Please avoid doing so. There's no way to predict which of the two attributes is actually used.
