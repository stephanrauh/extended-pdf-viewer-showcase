[customPdfViewer] allows you to replace the entire default viewer HTML with your own template. This gives you full control over the viewer structure: you can rearrange or remove the toolbar, sidebar, findbar, and any other UI element.

The template replaces everything except the PDF rendering itself. The <div id="viewer"> is where pdf.js renders the pages — you must include it along with its parent containers (#outerContainer, #mainContainer, #viewerContainer).

You also need <pdf-dummy-components> which provides hidden DOM elements that pdf.js expects.
